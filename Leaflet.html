Leaflet Trash Cans

I first learned about these from my grandmother who lives in Japan. There are many uses for these, such as using them for scrap pieces of trash, orange peels, bones from chicken wings, and much more! This is a convenient way to keep your trash contained and organized. It is a quick and easy method to keep your area clean and tidy while recycling old newspapers or magazines.

Estimated time: 5 - 10 minutes
